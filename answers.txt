Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: For one it helps manage code and data changes when working with a team. Secondly, we might also use Git for personal uses to store projects and code on the cloud easily rather than locally.
Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: It can be used in sports to analyze the attributes of certain players or tactics of entire teams. For example, when teams want to trade players it is extremely important to use data science to quantify their value and analyze their numbers to see if they fit with the team.
Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines two branches by creating a new merge commit that preserves the history of both branches. However, rebasing will just copy over the commits from one of the branches, so it's like overriding it.
